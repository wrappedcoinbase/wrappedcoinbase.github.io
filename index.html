Ecosystem

We’re building an ecosystem for tokenized assets, working with leading DeFi protocols, financial service providers, and investment firms to realize our vision.

CowSwap is the first trading interface built on top of CoW protocol. It guarantees users the best price by either: Matching the user with a fitting sell order or routing the order to the best liquidity venue on-chain.

https://swap.cow.fi/

Base is an optimistic rollup chain built by Coinbase. It emphasizes modular design and developer flexibility, allowing for custom chains built atop the Base network.

https://www.base.org

Balancer is a decentralized finance platform built on blockchain technology. It operates as an Automated Market Maker, automating portfolio management via liquidity pools. This lets users manage asset allocation and engage in crypto trading, utilizing the BAL-token as its primary cryptocurrency.

https://balancer.fi/

Gnosis, in addition to being a Chain and DAO, also invest in startups pushing the future of blockchain technologies.

https://www.gnosis.io

Aerodrome Finance is a decentralized trading platform and liquidity hub for the Base blockchain.

https://aerodrome.finance/

ParaSwap is a decentralized exchange aggregator that optimizes swaps for users through better prices, limited gas fees, and lower slippage.

https://www.paraswap.xyz/
